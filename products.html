Collaboration with Furniture Services

Our collaboration with furniture services (FaaS) is a great way to get the furniture you want without any hassle on your side. We have partnered with some of the leading furniture services in the industry to bring you high quality products at an optimal price. Whether you are looking for multiple ergonomic chairs, a standing desk, or any other office furniture, we have you covered. Our furniture services offer a wide range of products to suit every style and budget.

What if you want Hunchi without FaaS

If you already have your own office furniture, and only want the Hunchi service, that is absolutely possible! Simply indicate that you only want the Hunchi service. By buying only the Hunchi service, you will still receive all the applications and services, including the introductory presentation, installation, and ongoing maintenance.

The Product

The Hunchi is a small puppet placed on your desk next to the screen you are working on. Using ultrasound, the Hunchi will measure your current sitting positions. When you are sitting in the wrong position, the Hunchi will give a visual indication by bending its head forwards. When you have adjusted your sitting position, the Hunchi will also go back to its neutral position. In the accompanying app and desktop, the user can see your sitting posture progress over time. In the application, different Hunchi settings can also be changed to make the Hunchi feedback personalized for you.

End of Life

To provide for a sustainable future, our service includes identifying which components from the Hunchi can be reused and repurposed in a different Hunchi units. The components that cannot be used anymore will be separated and recycled in the collaborating recycling centres, ensuring a circular economy.
